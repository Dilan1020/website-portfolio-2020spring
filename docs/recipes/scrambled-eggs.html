These resturant-style eggs are great for a quick and healthy breakfest, or an mid-day meal

1.

Ingredients

  • 6 Eggs
  • 15 grams butter
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Chives
  • 1 TBs milk

2.

Preparation

Crack 6 cold eggs into a deep saucepan on high heat. Add the butter. For smaller batches, use a 2-to-1 eggs-to-butter ratio.

3.

Temperature Control

Stir continuously with, don't whisk, making sure to scrape the bottom of the pan.
After 30 seconds, take the pan off the heat. Keep stirring. After about 10 seconds, put back on the heat. Repeat for 3 minutes.

4.

Final

Finally, season the eggs lightly with salt and pepper. Plate with chopped chives.
