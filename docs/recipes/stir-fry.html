This vegtable stir fry is the perfect Chinese-restaurant-style dish that you can make at home

1.

Ingredients

  • 1 Lb broccoli
  • 8oz sliced muchrooms
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Vegtable oil
  • 3 cloves Garlic
  • 1 TBs ginger
  • 2 TBs sesame oil
  • 1/3 cup soy sauce
  • 1 TBs brown sugar
  • 1 cup flour
  • 1 cup vegetable broth

2.

Preperation

In a medium-large sauce pan, heat 1 tablespoon of oil and add mushrooms. When the mushrooms start to soften, add broccoli florets and stir-fry until the broccoli is tender.
Remove cooked mushrooms and broccoli from the pan and set aside.

3.

Saute

Add 1 tablespoon of vegetable oil to the pan and saute garlic and ginger. Add sesame oil, brown sugar, broth, soy sauce, and flour. Stir until smooth.

4.

Final

Return the vegetables to the sauce pan, stir until heated through.
Serve with hot rice or noodles.
