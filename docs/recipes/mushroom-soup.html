This creamy mushroom soup is a delicous meal for those looking for a healthy dish

1.

Ingredients

  • 4 Tbs butter
  • vegetable oil
  • 1/2 pound sliced mushrooms
  • 6 TBs flour
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • 2 cans chicken broth
  • 1 cup half-and-half cream

2.

Preperation

In a large saucepan, heat butter over medium-high heat, then saute mushrooms until tender.

3.

Mixing

Mix flour, salt, pepper and 1 can of chicken broth until smooth. Stir into sauting mushroom mixture until boiling. Cook and stir for about 2 minutes. Reduce heat and stir in cream. Then, simmer the uncovered soup for about 15 minutes, stirring occasionally.
